Who We Are

Hedgehogs is a family owned business in Las Cruces, NM. We have been in business since 1995. We are a leader in landscape design and maintenance services. Our professional staff and experienced team can take care of all of your landscape and irrigation needs.

NM Irrigation Contractor #57008

We are a licensed Irrigation Contractor in the State of New Mexico. (CID # 57008) This means we install sprinkler systems that meet both city and state code and keep you out of trouble. We guarantee that your system will work properly.

Commercial Pesticide Applicator #52215

We are licensed Commercial Pesticide Applicators through New Mexico Dept. of Agriculture. (NMDA # 52215) This means we will take all precautions when applying chemicals to your yard so you and your pets stay safe. We are able to obtain commercial pesticides that are not available to the homeowner.
